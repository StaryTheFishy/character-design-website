About the Website

What is Character Designs?

Character Designs is a website made by Jessica Dai, made to show and display the numerous character designs that were made!

Why was Character Designs made?

Character Designs was made for showing off and displaying character designs made by the creator of the website. However, we may make it so that other users can submit characters as well, with an account.

What is the purpose of Character Designs?

The main purpose of Character Designs is to show characters to a wide variety of people online, and show people what character design is like, and also let artists take inspiration from them!

About the Creator

Jessica Dai, the creator of Character Designs, is currently a student in college studying graphic design, hoping to be able do something related to media arts in the future. They enjoy making original characters, and wanted to show them, so they decided to create this website to display them! they hope to make it so that anonymous users can submit their character designs as well, to be displayed.
